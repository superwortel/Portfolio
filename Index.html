My origin
When i was 14 in middleschool i founded CortexArts together with Marc Frenken and some friends. after graduating, I enrolled into the Saxion University of Applied Sciences to follow the Creative Media and Game Technologies course. Currently I am a second year student.
My passion
There is always more to learn, to do, to create and to see. My aim is to not only help myself to discover everything the world has to offer and follow your passions but others aswell To me, games are a great way to share both knowledge and experiences with the people around us and i love being part of shaping worlds for others to explore.
My dream
Revolutionising education through gamification could be considered my life long dream of archieving. Making education personalised and enabling students to learn the way they want to learn with games would drasticly improve education. Motivating the students of the future to learn and explore their passions will lead to a better future for everyone.
